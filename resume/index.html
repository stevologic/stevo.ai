Executive profile

Stephen builds at the intersection of applied AI, cybersecurity, and product engineering. His public portfolio favors systems that make powerful capabilities understandable and reviewable: agents receive scoped context, security work requires proof, and users retain control over consequential actions.

The work ranges from security knowledge infrastructure and dependency research to agent workspaces, communication tools, and focused end-user products. Across those projects, the consistent pattern is practical experimentation followed by disciplined, usable delivery.
